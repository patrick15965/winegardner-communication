OVERVIEW
--------
Patrick participated in a full-day consulting engagement with software consultants Jordan and Casey at his masonry/construction company (Weingartner). The day involved interviews with multiple team members about operational challenges, software needs, and process improvements. Patrick was interviewed about his role bridging field operations and office management, discussed his daily workflows using FieldFlow, Teams, J Drive, and Outlook, and shared frustrations about doing PM work that takes him away from mentoring foremen and apprentices. The consultants also interviewed estimator Angel about the estimating process and bid handoff challenges, observed tension between estimating and operations teams, and discussed potential software solutions including AI-assisted estimating tools. Patrick offered to take the consultants on a job site visit to Ontario on Thursday. The consultants planned to work overnight creating mockups and prototypes to present the next morning.
